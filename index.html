Solutions U

We seek to unleash the agency and purpose of every student by connecting them with stories that reveal real world possibilities. Education in the 21st century is about learning how to identify, analyze and solve problems. The world is full of people and institutions working to advance change. Yet, these emerging ideas can be difficult to find...until now. SolutionsU connects educators and students to credible news stories about responses to the world's challenges, and to tools to help incorporate these stories in their teaching and learning.
